The Project

Mountain
Life
Project

This is not an adventure project. Not content.
Not a challenge to complete.

It is an ongoing practice.
A way of orienting life around the mountain.

Its rhythms. Its demands.
Its refusal to negotiate with ego or urgency.

The mountain is not a backdrop.
It is the condition under which everything else is understood.

Long-term and deliberate.
Not peak-bagging. Not performance.

What matters is the return.
Without expectation. Moving with the terrain, not against it.

Consistency over intensity.
The summit, when it comes, is incidental.
